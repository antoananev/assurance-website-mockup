FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my roof has storm damage?
Sometimes storm damage is obvious — missing shingles, dents from hail, or leaks inside your home. But a lot of the time, the damage is hidden and not visible from the ground. Even small issues like lifted shingles or subtle cracks can lead to serious problems later. That's why it's best to have a professional inspect your roof after any storm — at no cost to you.
Will my insurance cover the roof replacement?
In many cases, yes — especially if the damage was caused by a storm. We specialize in working with insurance companies and can help you through the entire claims process to ensure you get the coverage you deserve.
How much will I pay out of pocket?
The cost varies based on roof size, materials, and the extent of the damage. If your roof qualifies for an insurance claim, your out-of-pocket cost may be minimal — often just your deductible. We offer free inspections and estimates so you can make an informed decision with zero pressure.
How long does a roof replacement take?
Most residential roof replacements are completed in just one day. Our team works efficiently while maintaining top-quality workmanship and leaves your property clean and protected.
What types of roofing do you install?
We install a variety of roofing systems, including asphalt shingles, metal roofing, and flat roofing — all from top-rated manufacturers. We'll help you choose the best option based on your home's needs and your budget.
Do you offer warranties?
Yes! We back our work with strong labor warranties, and many of our roofing products come with manufacturer warranties as well. Your satisfaction and long-term protection are our top priorities.
